Fire Emblem: Heroes

The long-running hit Fire Emblem series comprises strategy/role-playing games with deep tactical elements and depth that increases with every move you make.

Play Now!

Meet some of the Heroes

Story Mode – Players play through the story of Heroes, following the adventures of Prince Alfonse, Princess Sharena, and the summoner Kiran. The main story is divided into Books, with a new Book introduced each year with a major version update to the game.

Alfonse

A prince of Askr Kingdom and member of the Order of Heroes. Kind and serious, and eager to maintain peace. A new character for Fire Emblem Heroes.

Sharena

A princess of Askr and Alfonse's sister. Cheerful and loves making friends—especially with Heroes. A new character for Fire Emblem Heroes.

Kiran

Kiran is a protagonist in Fire Emblem Heroes. Similar to Mark from Fire Emblem: The Blazing Blade, Kiran is a silent character who is an Avatar, representing the player in-game. Their primary role is as the summoner capable of bringing forth Heroes to fight for the Order of Heroes.

Anna

A veteran fighter who leads the Order of Heroes. Alfonse and Sharena's commander. Appears in Fire Emblem Heroes...and elsewhere.

Veronica

The young princess of the Emblian Empire. She aims to destroy the Kingdom of Askr by manipulating Heroes from other worlds. A new character for Fire Emblem Heroes.

Fjorm

The gentle second princess of Nifl, the Kingdom of Ice. Seeks revenge against Surtr. A new character for Fire Emblem Heroes.
